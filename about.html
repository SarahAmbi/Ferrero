ABOUT US

The roots of Ferrero's success go back to the forties. Back then, Piera and Pietro Ferrero, Michele Ferrero's parents, turned their confectionery shop in Alba into a small factory for confectionery. Encouraged by their success, the Ferrero family soon became the first Italian confectionery manufacturer to open offices and production sites abroad. In this way, the formerly small company grew into a truly international enterprise. A growth that was mainly due to the products "invented" by Pietro and his then very young son Michele. In addition, another key to success was the efficient Ferrero distribution network. It was built up by Giovanni Ferrero, Pietro Ferrero's brother, who died in 1949.Ferrero Rocher is a premium chocolate brand that has been delighting chocolate lovers for decades. Our company was founded in 1946 by Mr. Pietro Ferrero, a master pastry maker who had a passion for chocolate. He started by creating a revolutionary chocolate paste that could be used to make a wide range of chocolate confections. From there, Ferrero Rocher was born. Our company is based in Alba, Italy, where we continue to use the same traditional methods and high-quality ingredients that Mr. Ferrero used when he first started the company. We are committed to creating delicious chocolates that are made with the finest ingredients and crafted with passion by our skilled chocolatiers. Ferrero Rocher is known for its unique and irresistible combination of luscious, creamy, chocolaty filling surrounded by a delicate, crisp wafer, all enveloped in milk chocolate and finely chopped hazelnuts. Our chocolates are perfect for any occasion, whether you're looking for a gift for a loved one or simply want to treat yourself. We take pride in our commitment to quality and we are constantly innovating and expanding our product range to meet the needs of our customers. Today, Ferrero Rocher is enjoyed by chocolate lovers all over the world. We are proud to be a family-owned company that is dedicated to creating premium chocolates that bring joy to people's lives.
